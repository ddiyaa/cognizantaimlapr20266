Leave Policy:
Employees are eligible for 12 casual leaves per year.
Sick leave is 10 days per year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 working days in advance.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Probation Policy:
New employees have a probation period of 6 months.
Confirmation depends on performance review.

Notice Period Policy:
Employees must serve 60 days notice after resignation.
Buyout option depends on management approval.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain confidentiality of company data.
Harassment, discrimination, and data leakage are strictly prohibited.
